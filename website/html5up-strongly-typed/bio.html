Annie Souter

NASM Personal Trainer

AFAA Group Fitness Instructor

BA, Anthropology, University of California at Berkeley

MA, Education, California State University at Northridge

Annie has been in the fitness industry for nine years. She works at Bay Club in Walnut Creek and has also worked at Forma Gym in addition to running her own nutrition business. Annie is a NASM certified personal trainer and holds an AFAA group fitness certification, as well as many specialty certifications such as TRX, MOSSA Group Power, and High Fitness.

Annie strives to make every workout fun, challenging, and efficient. She enjoys getting to know her participants, whether they have never been to a gym before, or have been training for years. As a former classroom teacher, she brings excitement and creativity to her workouts, and clients never do the same workout twice! Annie’s goal is to help clients reach their weight loss or fitness goals with sustainable, balanced, and healthy lifestyle changes.

Cassie Sanders

AFAA Group Fitness Instructor

Rock the Ride Certified

High Fitness Certified

BA, Integrated Communications, Roosevelt University

Cassie has always been very active in the fitness community. In 2018, she decided to turn her passion for fitness into a profession. Cassie’s participants are motivated by her athleticism and endurance, bringing out the best in everyone in her classes. Cassie currently teaches Rock the Ride at Bay Club, Walnut Creek.

Growing up in Oahu, Hawaii, Cassie was immersed in competitive sports from a young age, excelling at many sports including varsity basketball. She spent many years in pharmaceutical sales until she decided to stay home to raise her kids while being an active volunteer in the school system. Once Cassie became a fitness professional it has been all systems go and she finds so much joy in helping others achieve their fitness goals while having a great time doing it. They say if you love what you do, you’ll never work a day in your life. This is definitely the case with Cassie as she couldn’t be any happier to do what she loves.
